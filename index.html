Invest in Kids

By making a donation to Howell Township Police Athletic League, you are contributing to the continuing efforts to improve the relationship between cops, kids, and the community. Additionally, your selflessness will enable us to offer more activities and programs for our youth and community in the future. We wouldn't be able to do anything without your help!
